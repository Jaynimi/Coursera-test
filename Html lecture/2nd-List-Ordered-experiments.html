Vegan Spaghetti Stirfry making procedure:
  1. Boil the Spag
  2. Wash the veggies
  3. Chop the veggies into different sizes as desired
  4. Put some oil into the pan and wait until it is hot
    1. Put the blended pepper into the hot oil first
    2. Season it
    3. Put in the chopped carrots and green beans after 5 minutes and stir thouroughly
    4. Put in the chopped red and green pepper right after
    5. Put in the other veggies after 2 minutes and let them fry for a bit while you stir at intervals
    6. Stir consistently after the stirfry is void of water
    7. Add your spaghetti and mix thoroughly
    8. Leave it to steam on low heat for 5 minutes
  5. Your Spaghetti Stirfry is ready to be served
